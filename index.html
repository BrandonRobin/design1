Manifesto

Design Is What You Make It

The insparation is all around as design is in all aspects of life. So its just up to us to observe it and make it your own.

Not All Projects Are Winners

Not all of my projects are winners. Sometimes you just can't get the project where you want it, but there are always highlights. At least one thing about it that resignates well.

Be Ambitious, Sometimes

I have always been ambitious in my design. I often attenpt things outside of my skill level or the allotted time. This just means that sometimes I will have to dial it back. Being aware of the ambition in the first place is the key.
